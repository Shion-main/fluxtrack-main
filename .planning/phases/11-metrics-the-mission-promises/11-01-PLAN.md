<objective>
Add the lateness computation to the pure aggregate layer (A3, D-01/D-02): one shared
formula helper `session_minutes_late`, one per-faculty Python fold `_lateness_map`, and
three new fields (`minutes_late_avg`, `late_sessions`, `chronic_late`) on both `FacultyRow`
and `Scorecard`, wired through `faculty_attendance` and `faculty_scorecard`. Redeems the
PROJECT.md core-value promise that lateness is "captured at the room level."

Purpose: This is the aggregate half of A3. The helper is THE single definition (the
lateness sibling of `_session_contribution`) that Plan 02's HR per-session CSV also imports,
so the faculty aggregate and the payroll export can never disagree.
Output: New pure/aggregate functions + dataclass fields + a dedicated mutation-resistant
test module + fixture support for known lateness deltas. No schema change, no migration.
