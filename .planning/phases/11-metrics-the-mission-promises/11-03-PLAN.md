<objective>
Add the verification-coverage metric (A6, D-04): what percentage of HELD sessions were physically
checker-verified, grouped by building and weekday, plus an explicit list of zero-coverage floors
(held sessions but no verification). Surfaced as new cards on the IFO dashboard. This redeems the
premise that a Checker's physical verification is the authoritative presence signal — a
checker-less week is no longer indistinguishable from a covered one.

Purpose: A6. A historical/management rollup, distinct from the live per-floor board the on-duty
checker already sees (do NOT duplicate web/checker.py). Built with the module's separate-query
discipline so a reverse-join can never inflate the held count.
Output: two new aggregates + dataclasses, dashboard wiring, a coverage template partial, a coverage
fixture, and mutation-resistant tests. No schema change.
