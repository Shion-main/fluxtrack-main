<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Browser POST -&gt; lifecycle controller/service | Role, proposed/persisted term id, details, distinct exact-name confirmation, reason, and acknowledgements are attacker-controlled/stale. |
| GET preflight -&gt; later POST | Display state can change before submission and is never authority. |

## STRIDE Threat Register (ASVS L1; high blocks)

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-12-02 | Elevation/Repudiation | lifecycle POSTs | high | mitigate | ifo_required plus service authorization, separate creation/transition exact confirmation, reason where required, atomic AuditLog. |
| T-12-03 | Tampering | stale preflight | high | mitigate | POST delegates to service lock/recompute; stale-state view tests. |
| T-12-01 | Tampering | combined/concurrent rollover | high | mitigate | Separate actions; activation service/DB invariant; no close-and-activate endpoint. |
| T-12-07 | Tampering/Data loss | legacy reset exposed in UI | critical | mitigate | No route/import/control invokes reset; source guard in lifecycle tests. |
</threat_model>
