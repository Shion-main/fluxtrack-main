| T-12-05 | Information Disclosure | live boards/locators | high | mitigate | Explicit authoritative ACTIVE predicate; same-date adversarial fixtures; no client selection. |
| T-12-03 | Tampering | stale page becomes archived | high | mitigate | Server-side re-gate immediately before transactional write; no trust in rendered state. |
