<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| GET query -&gt; report scope | Term/date/filter inputs are untrusted and may be missing, invalid, or outside term bounds. |
| HR links -&gt; pagination/export | Dropped term silently switches to ACTIVE and can disclose/widen history. |
| Streaming HR cursor -&gt; ORM | Per-row queries/writes can trigger MSSQL HY010 and partial responses. |

| T-12-10 | Tampering | out-of-range dates | medium | mitigate | Central parse/clamp and normalized links; invalid explicit term never widens. |

</threat_model>
