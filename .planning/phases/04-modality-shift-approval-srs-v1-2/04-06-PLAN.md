<objective>
Close the future-session gap: teach JOB-01 `materialize_sessions` to honor approved modality-shift requests so a recurring window that extends beyond the materialization horizon still applies to sessions created weeks later — born released (->Online) or born with the reserved room (->F2F), including any time-move (D-04/D-18, Pitfall 1).

Purpose: An approved "Thu & Fri for 3 weeks" request covers dates past the ~14-day horizon; those sessions don't exist yet at approval time. Without this hook, an approved shift silently drops the out-of-horizon sessions. The materializer is the only creator of future sessions, so the hook lives here.
Output: a born-released/born-assigned hook in the materialize command + two test suites.
</objective>
