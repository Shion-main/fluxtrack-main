## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-01 | Elevation | non-Dean / cross-department approve (IDOR) | high | mitigate | @dean_required + apply_approval/reject re-check role + request.department==dean.department + PENDING inside the transaction; DeanModalityAuthzTests proves both refusals |
| T-04-03 | Tampering | TOCTOU on approve | high | mitigate | View delegates to apply_approval whose availability re-check + transaction.atomic() own the write; the view never mutates state directly |
| T-04-07 | Tampering | partial apply surfaced as success | high | mitigate | A no-room ->F2F approval is surfaced as a terminal denial; the session is provably unchanged (D-07 REVISED) |
| T-04-05v | Input Validation | empty reject reason | low | mitigate | reject requires a non-empty reason; renders the queue partial at status=400 otherwise |
