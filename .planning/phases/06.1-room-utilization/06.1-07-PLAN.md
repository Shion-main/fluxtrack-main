<traps>
- **`build_csv` cannot be reused as-is.** It writes a module-level `HEADER`
  describing a `FacultyRow` (`report_render.py:55-67`). Room rows need their own
  builder. Reuse `csv_safe`, which is explicitly "the ONE neutralizer reused by
  every CSV export in the phase" -- do not write a second one.
- **Room codes and names come from an imported spreadsheet.** They are exactly the
  untrusted text `csv_safe` exists for. A room named with a leading formula
  trigger must export as literal text.
- **Export the full set, not the page.** The page's table is paged; the export
  runs the aggregate directly. `scorecard_csv` at `web/ifo.py:1344-1360` sets the
  precedent -- it runs the aggregate itself rather than reading paged context.
- **The export must not be wrapped in `safe_card`.** `safe_card` returns a
  `(value, error)` tuple for template rendering; a download endpoint has no card to
  degrade into. Let a genuine failure be a genuine error response, and keep the
  view GET-only.
</traps>
