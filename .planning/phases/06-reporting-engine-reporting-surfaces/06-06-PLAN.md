<objective>
Build the Dean reporting surface: a department-scoped dashboard (DEAN-04), department reporting + per-faculty scorecards (DEAN-02), CSV/PDF export (DEAN-03), all strictly read-only and department-scoped (DEAN-01).

Purpose: The Dean is the read-only, department-scoped consumer of the same aggregates IFO-09 uses. This surface is where the phase's central access-control requirement lives: a Dean must never reach another department's data, even with a crafted request. It reuses the aggregate layer, the render layer, and the shared scorecard/error templates.
Output: Dean dashboard + reports + scorecard + export views, templates, routes, nav link, and security-focused tests.
</objective>

| Dean -> department data | A Dean may read only their own department(s); a crafted faculty_id / report pk / department param must not cross the boundary |

Never trust a department id in GET/POST. |
