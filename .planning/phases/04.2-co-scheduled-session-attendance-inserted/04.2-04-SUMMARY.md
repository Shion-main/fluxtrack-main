## Decisions Made
- **The sweep needs no code, only a guard.** The present path makes siblings ACTIVE (Plan 01's `propagate_merged_present`) and the sweep only touches SCHEDULED, so a present merged group is naturally skipped; a no-event group shares one `scheduled_start` and is absented together. The regression test locks this so a future sweep edit can't silently fork the shared predicate.
- **Coverage classification is anchor-union-vs-group.** A group is CAUGHT when `{anchor} | merged_sibling_ids(anchor, rest)` equals the full member set; any leftover member shares only a distinct-both identity (different room AND course) and is MISSED — the exact criterion-#3 gap the audit must surface.
- **MISSED raises `CommandError`** (Django's idiomatic non-zero exit) after writing the escalation lines to stdout, so operator/CI runs fail loudly while still capturing the per-group detail.
