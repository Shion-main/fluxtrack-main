## Accomplishments
- Rewrote `import_offerings` to consume Plan 01's shared helpers instead of its own parsing: reads the offerings `.xlsx` by default via `xlsx.read_grid`, keeps `.csv` support via extension dispatch, and drops the old skip-virtual + GYM second-char rules (D1/D2).
- Per-meeting modality stamped by room via `modality_for_room` (D5); a blended course (e.g. COMM102/A101 with V415 + R415) yields an Online schedule for the virtual day and a scannable Blended schedule for the physical days.
- Instructor dedup by email then `normalize_name_key` (D7): the 57 blank-email rows collapse to exactly 10 accounts (each with an unusable password, flagged as unable to authenticate via Entra); 200 distinct instructors total.
- Roomless-physical sections routed to a shared "TBA" room in the "Unassigned" building; roomless-online to a shared "VTBA" Online placeholder (D9). Section labels are never treated as rooms.
- Replaced the flat stats report with a `reconcile()`-driven report: four bucket counts, the balanced `1042 + 44 + 14 + 111 = 1211 == total_rows [OK]` identity, 2021 real meetings, and WARNING-styled flag lists (typo rooms 404/516, Unassigned U101/U102, the 10 email-less instructors). Real runs additionally print live DB counts.
- Retired the dead `R3ParityTests` + `REAL_CSV` constant (stale pre-hardening numbers, permanently skipped); ENV-02 is now carried at full-term scale by the reconcile() + hardening tests.

## Decisions Made
- Roomless-online rows use a shared "VTBA" virtual placeholder in the Online building because `Schedule.room` is a required PROTECT FK — even an online-no-room section must point at a room; `modality_for_room` forces Online for it.
- Blank-email faculty usernames are derived deterministically (`fac-<slugify(name-key)>`) so dedup is idempotent across re-runs, not only within a single run's in-memory cache.
- Under `--building/--floor`, roomless rows are scoped out (their placeholders live in other buildings) so the CSV+filter regression (ImportPathTests) stays byte-identical.
- The command reports the reconciliation identity but does not raise on imbalance — enforcement lives in the tests and Plan 04's run/verify, per the plan.
